Hi, I am Colin, a Ph.D. specialized in Time Series processing and Machine Learning, and I work as a Data Engineer at Orange in the context of Cybersecurity. You are welcome to my web-portfolio.

My Ph.D. thesis took place at the Orange company, in collaboration with INRIA labs (LACODAM). In the context of infrastructure performances, I used Time Series datasets to forecast the performances of the servers for next day(s) to come. I proposed a Seasonal Time Series framework and several implementations of it. More details here.
